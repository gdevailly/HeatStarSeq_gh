Heat*seq

Correlation heatmaps for hight-througput sequencing experiments

by Guillaume Devailly, Anna Mantsoki, and Anagha Joshi

Heat*seq is an attempt to make genome-wide comparison of high throughput sequencing experiments easier. It currently works with gene expression data and with transcription factor ChIP-seq data (CAGE data coming soon). To get started, select HeatRNAseq or HeatChIPseq and read the instructions.
